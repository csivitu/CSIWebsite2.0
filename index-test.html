About

The Computer Society of India is the largest body of computer professionals in India. We are a group of skilled designers, developers and tech enthusiasts who engage in a lot of projects and hackathons. To help push technology forward, we organise a wide range of workshops, conferences, events and competitions both technical and non-technical.

02

Events

Laser Tag

graVITas

A premium event at graVITas, VIT's annual tech fest, LaserTag draws over 1,000 participants. Set in a neon-lit arena, players dodge, hide, and strike to score points and climb the leaderboard in this thrilling multiplayer experience. Team up, create unforgettable memories with friends, and emerge victorious!

Fork This

graVITas

ForkThis introduces participants to open-source development, offering hands-on experience with Git, GitHub, and version control. Designed for all skill levels, this expert-led workshop is a must-attend event for those looking to master open source, and take Hacktoberfest by storm!

DevSpace

Hackathon

Get ready to show your mettle in CSI's online hackathon, Devspace. Demonstrate your expertise across multiple tracks, including IoT, Fintech, Sports Analytics, Synthetix Protocol, L2 Optimism and more! Compete with over a thousand people for a jaw dropping array of prizes and show us all what you are made of.

Riddler

Online ARG

Riddler is an online puzzle hunt where logic meets the unknown. Each edition unveils a new immersive theme with brainbending riddles and factoids. With a legacy of challenging the brightest minds, this event pushes participants to out-think the competition in a relentless race to the top.

Byte Bistro

Seminar

In a rapidly evolving tech space, ByteBistro offers valuable insights into the latest trends, innovations, and challenges in the industry. The speakers share their personal experiences, and provide guidance to aspiring professionals. Come grab a byte at ByteBistro!

Init With CSI

Workshops

Init with CSI is a structured workshop series designed to provide hands-on experience across key tech domains. Through interactive sessions and practical implementation, the series builds a strong technical foundation in a collaborative learning environment.
